Not all speed tasks carry the same business impact. Focus first on what improves real user experience and conversion flow.

## Fix the largest blockers first

Start with image optimization, critical CSS, and reducing heavy script execution. These usually produce the fastest measurable gains.

## Measure before and after

Use Lighthouse and real-user metrics to confirm changes. Improvements should be visible in Largest Contentful Paint and Interaction to Next Paint.

## Keep performance in your publishing process

Every new page, script, or image can degrade speed over time. Add simple performance checks as part of your regular release workflow.
